About Our App

Please note that our app is still in alpha testing and user accounts are by invite only. Stay tuned for our beta release!

Welcome to the Hanshua Job Hunting App! This is a handy web application designed to take some of the stress out of the job hunt! We scrape relevant jobs from popular job sites like LinkedIn along with information about all the applicants to each of these listings. Our most powerful feature is a high-level analysis of every one of our listings in terms of the education level, area of expertise, and skillset of all other applicants. This means you can tell at a glance how suitable you are for a position or what areas you might need to improve in to be more competitive for certain types of positions. In addition, we offer the following features for a highly integrated job-hunt experience:

  • A handy, calendar-like way to keep track of all the jobs you might be interested in, the ones you have already applied for, and the ones for which you have a pending interview (hooray!).
  • A simple address book so you can keep all your job-related contacts in one place!
  • A simplified interface to generate a basic resume for upload to any major job site. The close integration of your resume with our job analysis feature makes it easy to identify relevant positions and areas of potential improvement.
